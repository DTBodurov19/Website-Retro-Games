Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Retro Gaming Club at MTU ("the Club"). Your privacy is important to us, and this
Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website
or engage with our services.


Information We Collect

  1. Personal Information:

    We may collect personal information such as your name, email address, student ID, and other
    contact details when you sign up for the club or events.

  2. Non-Personal Information:

    We collect non-identifiable information such as your IP address, browser type, and usage patterns to improve our website experience.

  3. Cookies:

    Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You may disable cookies in your
    browser settings, but some site features may be limited.

How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your membership in the Club.

  • To communicate about events, announcements, and activities.

  • To analyze website performance and improve content.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no system is entirely secure.
Please notify us of any suspected data breaches.


Your Rights

  • You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us.

  • You can unsubscribe from our communications at any time.

Contact Us

For questions or concerns, contact us at dimitar.bodurov@mycit.ie or our social media accounts.
